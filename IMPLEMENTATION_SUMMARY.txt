🎮 HOW TO PLAY CONNECT 4
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Objective: Get 4 pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)

1. Click a column button (⬇) to drop your piece
2. It falls due to gravity to the lowest available position
3. Players alternate turns
4. First to get 4-in-a-row wins the game
5. If board fills with no winner, it's a draw

Game Modes:
  🏠 LOCAL:   Two players share one device
  🤖 CPU:     You play RED, AI plays BLACK
  🌐 ONLINE:  Play with friends remotely (4-digit room code)
